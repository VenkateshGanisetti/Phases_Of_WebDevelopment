Web Development Process Overview

Web Development is just a process of building something, much like building a house. Just like you wouldn't start building a house by laying bricks randomly, you don't build a website by just writing code. There's a structured process we follow.

The Central Analogy: Building a Dream House
Throughout this session, we will compare building a website to building a custom-designed house. This will help you visualize each step.
