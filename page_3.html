Understanding Networks and Internet

What is a Network?

A network means "connection."

When two or more devices (mobiles/computers) are connected so they can share information, that is called a network.

"If two houses in a village are connected by a road, people can travel between them. Similarly, if two computers are connected, they can share information."

What is the Internet?

The Internet is like a giant network that connects computers and mobiles all over the world.
