Phase 6 - Maintenance

Maintenance is the ongoing process of keeping a website secure, functional, and relevant after it has been deployed.

"You've lived in the house for a while. You need to mow the lawn, fix a leaky faucet, maybe repaint a room after a few years, or even add a new extension."

Updates

Keeping the website's software, plugins, and security patches up-to-date to protect against hackers.

Bug Fixes

Fixing any new issues that users report to ensure the website continues to function correctly.

New Features

Adding new functionalities based on user feedback or business needs (e.g., adding a blog section later on).

Content Updates

Regularly adding new articles, products, or images to keep the site fresh and relevant.

Deliverable: An ongoing process of keeping the site healthy and improving it over time.
