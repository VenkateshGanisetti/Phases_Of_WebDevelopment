Why Do We Use Web Development?

Web development serves five main purposes that benefit individuals and organizations:

To Share Information

  • Schools share schedules, results, notices
  • Government shares schemes and services
  • News websites share latest updates

To Provide Services

  • Booking train tickets (IRCTC)
  • Paying electricity bills
  • Applying for jobs
  • Online shopping

For Communication

  • Social media (WhatsApp Web, Facebook)
  • Video calls and messaging
  • Connecting with friends and family

For Business

  • Companies sell products through websites
  • Hospitals provide online appointments
  • Hotels allow online room bookings

For Learning

  • YouTube classes
  • Online courses
  • College and school websites
