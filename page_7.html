Phase 1 - Planning and Requirement Analysis

The "What and Why" Phase

This is the foundation. Before you do anything, you need to know what you are building and why. It's about gathering all the necessary information.

Target Audience

Who is the website for? (e.g., Students, shoppers, professionals)

Website Purpose

What is the website's purpose? (e.g., To sell products, to provide information, a blog)

Required Features

What features are required? (e.g., A login system, a shopping cart, a contact form)

Deliverables

A detailed project document, a sitemap, and a project timeline

The House Analogy

Imagine you're building a house. You don't just start digging. You sit with an architect and discuss:

  • Who is the house for?
  • What features are needed?
  • What's the budget and timeline?
